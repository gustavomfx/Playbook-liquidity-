# Engine

## Engine v1

The Engine is the core logic of Playbook Liquidity.

It defines the sequential decision process Gustavo follows before taking a trade.

The Engine does not predict the market.

It verifies whether Gustavo is following his own trading process.

---

## Decision #001

The Engine is sequential.

Gustavo cannot evaluate a lower timeframe before completing the higher timeframe context.

---

## Decision #002

Each stage has an exit condition.

Gustavo cannot move to the next stage until the current stage is clear.

---

# Stage 1 — HTF Context

## Objective

Build the market narrative before looking for an entry.

---

## Core Questions

### 1. Where are we?

- Main Push
- Complex Pullback
- Continuation

### 2. Are we mitigating a previous Main Push?

- Yes
- No

### 3. What points can be induced?

Identify the liquidity areas that price may target before continuing.

### 4. What is the most likely continuation after that induction?

- Buy continuation
- Sell continuation
- Still unclear

---

## Exit Condition

Gustavo can only move to M15/M30 after clearly answering:

- Am I in Main Push, Complex Pullback or Continuation?
- Are we mitigating a previous Main Push?
- What liquidity do I expect to be induced?
- What is the expected narrative after that induction?
